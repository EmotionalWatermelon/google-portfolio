SSA market perspective

Mobile is everything

71% of all digital ad spend in Africa flows through mobile in 2026. SMBs that are not mobile-optimised are invisible to most of their potential customers. Google's Performance Max is built for exactly this reality.

The market is accelerating

South Africa's digital ad market reaches $2.4 billion in 2026, growing at 12.2% annually and forecast to hit $3.67 billion by 2029. The SMB segment is the fastest-moving part of this growth.

Trust is the real barrier

Many SSA SMBs are advertising online for the first time. Their hesitation is not budget — it is confidence. They need a trusted advisor who understands their market, not just their metrics.

The competitive gap

Meta and TikTok currently dominate SMB digital marketing across Nigeria, Kenya, and South Africa. Google's opportunity is in helping SMBs understand the performance advantage of search and Performance Max over pure social.

How I would solve the 40% activation problem

40% of businesses that sign up for Google Ads never activate their account. This is not a product problem — it is a trust and confidence problem. Here is how I would approach reducing that number.

1

Early intervention within 24 hours

The drop-off happens in the first 48 to 72 hours. A proactive personalised outreach call before they disengage — not after — is the first move. Get there before the doubt sets in.

2

Simplify the path to first success

Do not present the full product suite at activation. Get one Performance Max campaign live with one simple goal. Small wins build the confidence to invest more.

3

Set expectations for what month one looks like

Most non-activation is fear of wasting money. A short consultative call that explains realistic results on their budget removes the fear and replaces it with a plan.
